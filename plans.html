Choose Your Plan

Healthy Weight Loss Plan

✔ Lose weight safely (1–2 lbs per week)

Daily Guidelines

  • Eat whole foods (fruit, vegetables, lean protein, whole grains)
  • Drink 8+ cups of water
  • Avoid sugary drinks
  • Choose high-protein meals
  • Get 7–9 hours of sleep

Workout Plan

  • 30 minutes of walking or light cardio daily
  • Strength training 3x per week
  • Stretch 10 minutes per day

Example Meal Plan

  • Breakfast: Eggs + fruit
  • Lunch: Chicken, rice, vegetables
  • Dinner: Fish, potatoes, salad
  • Snacks: Greek yogurt, nuts, apples

Healthy Weight Gain Plan

✔ Gain weight safely (0.5–1 lb per week)

Daily Guidelines

  • Eat more calories than you burn
  • Have 5–6 meals per day
  • Choose calorie-dense healthy foods
  • Drink smoothies with fruit + yogurt or milk
  • Increase protein intake

Workout Plan

  • Strength training 3–4x per week
  • Focus on muscle building (light cardio only)
  • Rest well between workouts

Example Meal Plan

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal + peanut butter + banana
  • Lunch: Beef or chicken pasta
  • Dinner: Salmon, rice, veggies
  • Snacks: Protein shakes, nuts, granola
