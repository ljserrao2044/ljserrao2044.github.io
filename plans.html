Healthy Weight Loss Plan

• Calorie goal: 300–500 below maintenance
• High protein diet (120–180g/day)
• Daily steps: 8,000–12,000
• Strength training: 3–4× per week
• Avoid sugary drinks & junk food

Healthy Weight Gain Plan

• Calorie goal: 300–500 above maintenance
• High calorie healthy foods (rice, oats, nuts)
• Protein: 100–160g/day
• Strength training: 4–5× per week
• Limit cardio to avoid burning extra calories
